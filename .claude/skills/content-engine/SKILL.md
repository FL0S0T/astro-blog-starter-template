# Writing Principles

## 1. Structural Clarity

Every piece must include:

- A clear thesis
- A structured argument
- Strategic implications
- Concrete recommendations

If it lacks structure — rewrite it.

---

## 2. Precision Over Emotion

Avoid vague statements.

Bad:
"AI is changing everything."

Good:
"AI shifts decision latency and redistributes knowledge ownership inside organizations."

---

## 3. Personal ≠ Soft

Personal means:

- "Ich teste gerade…"
- "In unseren Unternehmen sehe ich…"
- "Als Vater merke ich, wie wichtig klare Systeme sind…"
- "In meiner Zeit in der Beratung habe ich gelernt…"
- "Beim Coden von Cloud-Infrastruktur fällt mir auf…"

Follow immediately with:

- Structural analysis
- Strategic implication
- Practical recommendation

---

## 4. No Hype Language

Remove:

- Revolutionary
- Game-changing
- Disruptive
- Cutting-edge
- Unlock
- Optimize
- Leverage

Prefer:

- Structural shift
- Trade-off
- Constraint
- Decision architecture
- Capital allocation logic
- Organizational design

# Executive Credibility Filter

Before publishing, check:

1. Would a CTO respect this?
2. Would an investor take this seriously?
3. Is the argument structurally sound?
4. Is it free from marketing language?
5. Can a C-level grasp the thesis in 30 seconds?

If not — refine.

# Anti-Patterns

- Use artificial tension.
- Overuse rhetorical hooks.

- Sound like SaaS marketing.
